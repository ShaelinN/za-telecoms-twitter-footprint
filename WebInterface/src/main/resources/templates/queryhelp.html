Query 1: Chart page

This query will show a bar graph and a pie chart of the market share of the 4 different companies to be displayed. This query will show you who has the most and least interactions

Query 2: Companies Page

This page will simply show you how many and which companies we are working with

Query 3: Number of tweets

This page will tell you the number of tweets sent by the company you have chosen. Once clicked you will have 4 buttons that will appear below that will allow you to choose a company. Clicking the button of the company you want will then take you to a page contaning the number of tweets.

Query 4: Frequently Mentioned User

This page will show you the users that are most actively interacting with the company chosen. Once clicked you will have 4 buttons that will appear below that will allow you to choose a company. Clicking the button of the company you want will then take you to a page where the statistics of all the mentioned users are

Query 5: Popular Hashtags

This option will simply take you to a page that has a list of all the most popular hashtags and how popular they are

Query 6: Mentions per company

This option will take you to a page that displays all the times that the company was mentioned

Query 7: Company Sentiment

This option will take you to a page that shows the different general feelings that customers have towards the chosen company

Query 8: Analysis

This page will tell you our team's thoughts and conclusions on the data that we acquired
