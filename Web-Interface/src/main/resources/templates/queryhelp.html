Help

Query 1: Chart page

This query will show a bar graph and a pie chart of the market share of the 4 different companies to be displayed. This query will show you who has the most and least interactions

Query 2: Companies Page

This page will simply show you how many and which companies we are working with

Query 3: Number of tweets

This page will tell you the number of tweets sent by the company you have chosen. Once you click this option, below 4 buttons will appear that will allow you to choose which company you would like to see the number of tweets for, once you have chose a company you will then go to the page with their statistics

Query 4: Frequently Mentioned User

This page will show you the users that are most actively interacting with the company chosen. Once clicked you will again have 4 buttons appear that will allow you to choose a company. Clicking the button of the company you want will then take you to a page where the statistics of all the mentioned users are

Query 5: Popular Hashtags

This option will simply take you to a page that has a list of all the most popular hashtags and how popular they are

Query 6: Mentions per company

This option will take you to a page that displays all the times that the company was mentioned

Query 7: User Sentiment

This option will take you to a page that shows the different general feelings that customers have towards a chosen company

Query 8: Analysis

This page will tell you our teams thoughts on the data gathered
